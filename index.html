Who We Are
NISFA Media House — NMH
NISFA Media House (NMH) is Nigeria's dedicated agribusiness, trade and market intelligence media platform — built to serve farmers, traders, exporters, investors and agribusiness operators with timely, credible and actionable information. Starting as a structured YouTube channel, NMH is on a clear roadmap to become NMH TV: a full dedicated agribusiness television network serving Nigeria and the wider African continent.
